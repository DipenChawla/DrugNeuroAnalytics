Carbamazepine

Carbamazepine is used to prevent and control seizures. This medication is known as an anticonvulsant or anti-epileptic drug.

Acetazolamide

It is used in the treatment of glaucoma, drug-induced edema, heart failure-induced edema, epilepsy and in reducing intraocular pressure after surgery.It has also been used in the treatment of altitude sickness.

  • Reviews

Divalproex

Divalproex sodium is used to treat various types of seizure disorders. Divalproex sodium is sometimes used together with other seizure medications.

  • Reviews

Ethosuximide

This medication is used alone or with other medications to prevent and control a certain type of seizure (absence or petit mal seizure). It works by controlling the abnormal electrical activity in the brain that occurs during a seizure.

  • Reviews

Phenobarbital

Phenobarbital is a barbiturate, nonselective central nervous system depressant which is primarily used as a sedative hypnotic and also as an anticonvulsant in subhypnotic doses.

  • Reviews

Phenytoin

It is an anti-seizure medication.It is useful for the prevention of tonic-clonic seizures and partial seizures, but not absence seizures.

  • Reviews

Primidone

Primidone belongs to a class of drugs known as barbiturate anticonvulsants. It works by controlling the abnormal electrical activity in the brain that occurs during a seizure..

Valporic Acid

This medication is used to treat seizure disorders, mental/mood conditions (such as manic phase of bipolar disorder), and to prevent migraine headaches.
