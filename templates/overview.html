Overview

Medical speciality concerned with the care of patients dying due to adverse drug reactions

        Creating new drugs is very time consuming and costly process which requires lot of efforts and analysis of previous drugs related. Adverse drug reactions are among the most common causes of death these days. Expensive clinical trials are not sufficient to uncover all of the adverse reactions a drug may cause, necessitating systems for monitoring the effects of medical drugs after they have been licensed for use. So,Drugs adverse reactions and effectiveness of drugs plays an important role in discovering new or enhancing the previous drugs to give expected results for treatments.On the other side of the healthcare spectrum, users commonly reach out to various social media platforms and blogs in order to convey their views about an administered drug. Extracting Adverse Drug Reactions (ADE) from such comments provides a new direction to the field of healthcare, which is already in the midst of a data explosion, thanks to the growth of social media as a tool for healthcare analysis. In this website, we have analyzed patient reviews on social media platforms about drugs they have been taking for their treatments and we have also generated brief summaries and reports that can help in pharma industry for measuring efficiencies.Our website will be useful for both medical professionals and patients. The website contains a concise summary of the drug under consideration as well as shows the top reviews in each category i.e effective, non-effective and adverse. This would save the efforts of the patients required for checking multiple websites to get a brief about the drug administered to him/her. Besides, the user would also get an idea about the commonly experienced effects for the drug. Based on the comparative report for the effects of drugs for the same disorder, medical practitioners can also suggest alternative drugs for the one's which are not much effective.
