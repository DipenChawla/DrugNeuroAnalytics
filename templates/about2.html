About WeCare

Medical specialty concerned with the care of patients dying due to adverse drug reactions

Adverse drug reactions are among the most important causes of death nowadays. Expensive clinical trials are also not sufficient to identify all the adverse reactions of a particular drug. So we have developed a system that users's experience with the drugs to find out the adverse reactions caused by that drug.

User Reviews

We have monitored about more than 3000 reviews sharing experiences of various users.

Websites

The reviews obtained were from various websites i.e. everydayhealth.org, drugs.com, webmd.

Visualisation

To compare the adverseness of various drugs, we have provided various types of visualisation like stacked bar chart, grouped bar chart and pie chart.

Summarization

After analyzing the user reviews, we have generated a summary consisting of top ranked reviews, top efective words and top adverse words.
